Entry Fee Information

Thank you for your interest in our photography contest. To enter the contest, there is a $25 entry fee. The entry fee helps us cover the cost of running the contest and provides great prizes for the winners.

To submit your photo, please fill in the form on the submit photo page and follow the instructions for payment. You will receive a confirmation email once your entry has been received.
